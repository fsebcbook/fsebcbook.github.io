Introduction

This book covers five shades of emerging business topics that can be used in Management Schools as Case Studies. Each topic comprises of three case studies and freely available for download and use with proper citation. Each article were either personally or collaboratively written by the authors and are distributed in Elsevier SSRN Electronic Journal with unique Digital Object Identifier.

DOWNLOAD

The fsebcbook is available as individual chapters that you can freely download as Elsevier SSRN working papers. Each chapter is shared under a Creative Commons CC-BY-NC-ND license.

We will be making improvements based on community feedback. It will be released in print in late 2021, but this electronic version will remain publicly available.

When the print version is available, you can buy it here.
